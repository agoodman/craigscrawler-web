Much more to come!

We have a lot planned for the future, but coming later this summer, we'll be adding:

  • Saved searches - the ability to set up the searches you use most. One click to see live results.

  • Region targeting - filter down to CraigsList's most granular regions to narrow your results list.
  • Multi-region searches - see results from as many regions as you want to explore.
